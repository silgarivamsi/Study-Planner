About Smart Study Planner

Our Objective

Smart Study Planner & Reminder System is designed specially for college students to manage their academic activities efficiently.

  • Plan daily, weekly, and monthly study schedules
  • Manage subjects, assignments, and exams easily
  • Improve time management and reduce exam stress

This system helps students stay organized, focused, and productive throughout the semester.

FEATURES

Secure Login

User authentication system ensures secure access to personal study data and protects user information.

Subject Management

Add, edit, and manage subjects easily to keep academic activities organized and structured.

Study Scheduling

Create daily, weekly, and monthly study plans to improve time management and productivity.

Task Tracking

Track assignments and tasks with deadlines and mark them as completed for better organization.

Smart Reminders

Receive reminders and notifications for important exams, assignments, and study sessions.

Progress Reports

Monitor academic performance with visual progress tracking and detailed reports.
